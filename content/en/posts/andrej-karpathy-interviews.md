## About Andrej Karpathy

Andrej Karpathy is one of the most influential researchers in artificial intelligence, spanning academia, engineering, and education:

- **OpenAI founding member** (2015–2017), involved in early R&D of GPT-1/GPT-2
- **Tesla AI Senior Director** (2017–2022), leading the Autopilot/FSD computer vision architecture
- **Founder of Eureka Labs**, dedicated to AI-native educational products
- **Top AI educator**, whose Neural Networks: Zero to Hero video series has influenced millions of learners

**Key Quotations:**


> "AGI is still a decade away — we're summoning ghosts, not building animals." — October 2025 Dwarkesh Podcast

> "Software is changing again" — The Software 3.0 era, where neural networks become the new programming paradigm

## Key Takeaways

- Karpathy places the AGI timeline "beyond a decade," and his famous analogy of "summoning ghosts, not building animals" profoundly reveals the current essence of AI development
- He is the most influential evangelist in AI education; the Zero to Hero video series is essential viewing for an introduction to deep learning
- From Tesla Autopilot to Eureka Labs, Karpathy's career trajectory mirrors AI's complete journey from academic research to large-scale productization
- The Software 3.0 concept is his core framework: neural networks replacing traditional code as the new programming paradigm
