## Three Counter-intuitive Conclusions

### 🔴 Conclusion 1: Open-source models are the true disruptors of the enterprise market

DeepSeek topping Trending proves:

> **It's not about who is the strongest, it's about who is the cheapest.**

GPT-4 leads in performance, but DeepSeek's price makes US SMBs unable to say no.

### 🔴 Conclusion 2: AI note-taking tools are more of a necessity than AI writing tools

Grammarly only ranks 6th, but Granola ranks 2nd.

Explanation: **Saving repetitive labor is more urgent than improving writing quality.**

### 🔴 Conclusion 3: AI companies are eating into traditional software market share

Vercel/Netlify/Canva/Figma represent the "previous generation winners".

But Granola, Perplexity, Higgsfield represent the "new era of AI-native applications".
